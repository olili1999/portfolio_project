About Tennis Frands

Introduction

Hi! I'm Oliver, a student at the University of Michigan. I have created Tennis Frands because I've realized that tennis isn't a very accessible sport. Whether it be the weird lingo or the high cost for equipment, lessons, and court time, it's difficult for people to get into tennis. I'm hoping that this website will increase accessibility of the sport and the connections made through the sport.
